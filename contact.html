Tickets

How far in advance should I book Machu Picchu?

  • At least 3–4 weeks ahead in low season; 2–3 months in peak season (June–August).
  • Book at the official government portal: machupicchu.gob.pe
Altitude

What should I do about altitude sickness?

  • Spend 2 full days acclimatizing in Cusco before any strenuous activity.
  • Stay hydrated, avoid alcohol the first night, and drink coca tea.
Budget

How much does a week in Peru cost?

  • Budget travellers can manage on ~$35–50 USD per day including accommodation, food, and local transport.
  • Factor in big ticket items: Machu Picchu entry (~$50), Inca Trail permit (~$600).
